Billy Sharp

The man who scores goals

Career Achievements:

  • 1986 - Born in Sheffield, England.
  • 2004 - Breaks into Sheffield United's senior XI.
  • 2005 - Goes on loan at Rushden & Diamonds scoring 16 goals in 9 appearances.
  • 2006 - Signs permanently for Scunthorpe United scoring 82 goals in 53 appearances.
  • 2007 - Returns to Sheffield United for around 2 million GBP in a deal which saw Jonathan Forte transfer to Scunthorpe United. .
  • 2009 - Loaned to Doncaster Rovers for 2 years scoring 25 goals in 49 appearances.
  • 2012 - Signs for Southampton in a 1.8 million GBP move
  • 2013 - Loaned out to Nottingham Forest.
  • 2014 - Loaned out to Reading & Doncaster Rovers.
  • 2015 - Joins Leeds United on loan.
  • 2015 - Joins Sheffield United and continues to score goals.
